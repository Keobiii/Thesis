About Vikings

In the Vikings Sports PH Program we give our players the opportunity to play in different tournaments across Metro Manila. The Smart Spike, Viking Cup PH, The British School Manila Sports Tournaments, and the Spike and Serve Volleyball Academy Youth Developmental Tournaments are some of the organized games that the Vikings have played in. The club has also played friendly matches with different teams with the same vision of development and competition. Do you see yourself playing for us? Give it a try.
